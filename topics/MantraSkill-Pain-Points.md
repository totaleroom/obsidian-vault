## Key Themes

1. **Ghosting** — HR hilang setelah janji follow-up
2. **Credentials vs Reality gap** — GPA/certs tidak menjamin dapat kerja
3. **Age discrimination** — 30+ dianggap "sulit catch up"
4. **Inappropriate interview questions** — hal-hal tidak relevan
5. **Volume without success** — ngirim 100+ lamaran, 0 response
6. **Admin burden** — terlalu banyak dokumen sebelum interview
7. **Mental health impact** — "nyaris gila", burnout

## Content Angles Based on Pain Points

### Angle 1: "3.9 GPA, 3 Internships, Still Rejected"
> Hook: "Gue punya 3.9 GPA, 3 magang, 5 sertifikasi. Tetap ditolak."
> Value: ATS scanning CV sebelum human sees it. GPA doesn't matter if CV never gets read.

### Angle 2: "15,981 Lamaran, 56 Callbacks"
> Hook: "Orang ini ngirim almost 16,000 lamaran. Kita ngirim berapa?"
> Value: It's not about volume. It's about targeting and CV optimization.

### Angle 3: "HR Says 7 Days, Then Ghosts"
> Hook: "HR janji 7 days. Hari ke-8, nggak ada kabar."
> Value: Ghosting is a system problem, not a you problem. But there are ways to get noticed.

### Angle 4: "High GPA = Rejected"
> Hook: "Ternyata GPA 3.5+ bisa jadi disadvantages. Hiring manager admits why."
> Value: Soft skills + CV format matters more than grades.

### Angle 5: "Nyaris Gila dari Penolakan"
> Hook: "Di-Ghost, ditolak, di-Ghost lagi. Psikologis ampe nyaris gila."
> Value: You're not broken. The system is broken. Here's how to work with it.

### Angle 6: "Fresh Grad 9 Bulan Nganggur"
> Hook: "9 bulan setelah wisuda, belum dapat kerja pertama."
> Value: It's not you. It's the system. But there's a way to break in.
