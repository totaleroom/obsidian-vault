## Anti-AI Audit Checklist (per post)

Before posting, scan for:
- [ ] No "pivotal, crucial, testament, showcase"
- [ ] No "In order to, Due to the fact that"
- [ ] No em dashes (use commas or period)
- [ ] Varied sentence length (short + medium + short)
- [ ] First-person present ("gue/gw" or implied)
- [ ] Opinions present (not just facts)
- [ ] Specific details (35→87, 850+, 7 hari)
- [ ] Hook in first line
- [ ] No filler phrases
- [ ] Reads like a person, not a Wikipedia article
