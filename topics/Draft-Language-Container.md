# Draft Language Container

> Sistem panduan menulis yang tidak terlihat seperti AI. Berdasarkan 29 pola anti-AI + prinsip hook + craft songwriting + psikologi persuasif.

**Tujuan:** Buat setiap draf yang keluar terasa seperti ditulis manusia — dengan pendapat, ritme varied, dan jiwa. Tidak ada yang terasa "dibuat oleh AI."

---

## Golden Rules

1. **Punya pendapat** — bukan sekadar melaporkan fakta
2. **Ritme varied** — kalimat pendek, panjang, campuran
3. **Gunakan "I"** saat sesuai — jujur, bukan formal berlebihan
4. ** spesifik** — detail konkret > klaim kosong
5. **Hapus penanda AI** — patterns yang betray bahwa teks dibuat oleh LLM
6. **Tambahkan jiwa** — Personality, humor, edge, acknowledge uncertainty

## Anti-Patterns: 29 Tanda Teks AI

### Category 1: Konten — Inflation & Promosi

| # | Pattern | Example | Fix |
|---|---------|---------|-----|
| 1 | **Significance inflation** — kata-kata yang exaggerated importance | "marking a *pivotal moment*", "a *vital role*", "stands as a testament" | Tulis apa adanya. Tidak perlu embellish. |
| 2 | **Media coverage mention** — listing media mentions tanpa konteks | "cited in The NY Times, BBC, FT" | Pilih satu, beri konteks. Tidak perlu list. |
| 3 | **-ing phrases** — superficial analyses dengan -ing endings | "highlighting, underscoring, fostering, showcasing" | Hapus -ing phrases. Tulis langsung. |
| 4 | **Promotional language** — words like "breathtaking, stunning, vibrant, groundbreaking" | "nestled in the heart of..., rich cultural heritage" | Neutral. Deskriptif, tidak promotional. |
| 5 | **Vague attributions** — "Experts believe, Industry reports, Some critics argue" | Tanpa sumber spesifik | Src spesifik atau hapus. |

### Category 2: Bahasa — Vocabulary Overuse

| # | Pattern | Strike | Use Instead |
|---|---------|--------|-------------|
| 6 | **AI Vocabulary** — kata yang terlalu sering di AI text | `crucial, delve, pivotal, showcase, underscore, testament, landscape, tapestry, foster, enhance, garnish, interplay` | `key, explore, core, show, reveal, is, area, remove, build, improve, add, mix` |
| 7 | **Copula Avoidance** — menghindari "is/are" dengan elaborate constructions | "serves as, stands as, represents, functions as" | Gunakan `is`, `has`, `does` langsung |
| 8 | **Negative Parallelisms** — "Not only...but also", "It's not just about...it's about" | "It's not just about speed; it's about quality" | Langsung tulisIntinya tanpa struktur negatif |
| 9 | **Rule of Three** — memaksa ideas jadi grup tiga | "innovation, inspiration, and industry insights" | Tulis apa yang ada, tidak dipaksakan 3 |
| 10 | **Elegant Variation** — synonym cycling karena repetition penalty | "protagonist → main character → central figure → hero" | Ulang kata yang sama jika perlu |
| 11 | **False Ranges** — "from X to Y" yang tidak ada scale | "from the Big Bang to dark matter" | Tulis yang aktual saja |
| 12 | **Passive Voice** — hiding the actor | "No configuration file needed" | "You don't need a config file" |

### Category 3: Style — Visual Patterns

| # | Pattern | Example | Fix |
|---|---------|---------|-----|
| 13 | **Em dash overuse** | Terlalu banyak — dalam satu paragraf | Gunakan koma, period, atau parenthesis |
| 14 | **Boldface overuse** | Bold pada setiap phrase | Bold hanya untuk emphasis yang benar-benar penting |
| 15 | **Inline-header lists** | "- **User Experience:** ..." | Tulis sebagai paragraph natural |
| 16 | **Title Case in headings** | "## Strategic Negotiations And Global Partnerships" | Sentence case: "## Strategic negotiations and global partnerships" |
| 17 | **Emojis** | 🚀 💡 ✅ di headings | Hapus. Text-based only. |
| 18 | **Curly quotes** | "text" | Straight quotes: "text" |

### Category 4: Communication — Chatbot Artifacts

| # | Pattern | Example | Fix |
|---|---------|---------|-----|
| 19 | **Collaborative artifacts** | "I hope this helps!, Certainly!, You're absolutely right!" | Hapus. Langsung ke inti. |
| 20 | **Knowledge disclaimers** | "as of my last training update, based on available information" | Hapus. Tulis seolah-olah kamu tahu. |
| 21 | **Sycophantic tone** | "Great question! Excellent point!" | Neutral, tidak memuji. |

### Category 5: Filler & Hedging

| # | Pattern | Strike | Use |
|---|---------|--------|-----|
| 22 | **Filler phrases** | "In order to achieve, Due to the fact that, At this point in time" | "To achieve, Because, Now" |
| 23 | **Excessive hedging** | "could potentially possibly be argued that" | Langsung. "may" atau langsung nyatakan. |
| 24 | **Generic positive conclusions** | "The future looks bright. Exciting times lie ahead!" | Akhiri dengan fakta atau next step konkret. |
| 25 | **Hyphenated pairs overuse** | "cross-functional, data-driven, decision-making" secara konsisten | Consistent tapi tidak uniformly hyphenated. |
| 26 | **Persuasive authority tropes** | "The real question is, At its core, What really matters" | Langsung aja. Tidak perlu dramatis. |
| 27 | **Signposting** | "Let's dive in, Here's what you need to know, without further ado" | Tidak perlu announce. Lakukan saja. |
| 28 | **Fragmented headers** — heading + 1 line paragraph yang hanya restates heading | "## Performance\n\nSpeed matters." | Langsung mulai content tanpa warm-up sentence. |

## Hook Strategy

### Apa Itu Hook?

**Hook** = line yang orang ingat, hum, repeat. Biasanya ada di:
- First line — langsung ambil perhatian
- Last line of opening section — close dengan impact
- Title/core phrase — yang jadi memorable

### Types of Hooks

```
1. PROVOCATIVE QUESTION
   "What if everything you believed about productivity was wrong?"

2. BOLD STATEMENT
   "Most people are using ChatGPT completely wrong."

3. SPECIFIC DETAIL
   "I deleted 40% of my Notion workspace last week. Here's what happened."

4. CONTROVERSIAL TAKE
   "The 'follow your passion' advice is career poison for most people."

5. NARRATIVE OPENER
   "Three weeks ago I almost quit. Here's what changed my mind."

6. PATadox
   "To be more productive, I started doing less."

7. SCARCITY/Urgency
   "The window for learning AI at this price closes in 6 months."
```

### Hook Mechanics (dari Songwriting Craft)

- **Prosody**: hook harus align dengan emotion + struktur. Kalimat pendek di hook.
- **Repeatability**: bisa di-screenshot, di-quote, di-hurm
- **Specificity**: "40%" lebih strong dari "a lot"
- **Tension**: buat pertanyaan yang belum answered — orang akan scroll untuk tahu

### Where to Put Hooks

```
Opening     → Grab attention immediately
Between sections → Re-engage reader
End of section   → Make it memorable before transition
Closing     → Leave them with something to think about
```

## Voice & Rhythm

### Soulless vs. Human

| Soulless (AI) | Human |
|--------------|-------|
| Same sentence length throughout | Varied — short, medium, long |
| Neutral reporting | Opinions present |
| No uncertainty acknowledgment | "I'm not sure about..." or "This is weird but..." |
| No first-person | "I keep thinking about..." |
| No humor/edge | Occasionally wry, risky, specific |
| Reads like Wikipedia | Reads like a person talking |

### How to Add Soul

1. **Acknowledge complexity** — "This is impressive but also kind of unsettling"
2. **Use first-person** — "I keep coming back to...", "Here's what gets me..."
3. **Let some mess in** — tangents, asides, half-formed thoughts are human
4. **Be specific about feelings** — "something unsettling" not "concerning"
5. **Vary rhythm** — short punchy, then longer that takes time
6. **Have opinions** — react to facts, not just report them

### Before → After Example

**Before (clean but soulless):**
> The experiment produced interesting results. The agents generated 3 million lines of code. Some developers were impressed while others were skeptical. The implications remain unclear.

**After (has pulse):**
> I genuinely don't know how to feel about this one. 3 million lines of code, generated while the humans presumably slept. Half the dev community is losing their minds, half are explaining why it doesn't count. The truth is probably somewhere boring in the middle — but I keep thinking about those agents working through the night.

## Proses Review: 10-Step Anti-AI Audit

```
1. Read draft aloud
   → If it sounds like a Wikipedia article, fail.

2. Scan for significance inflation
   → "pivotal", "testament", "landmark", "groundbreaking" = CUT

3. Scan for AI vocabulary (tabel #6)
   → Strike each instance, replace with simpler word

4. Check copula avoidance
   → "serves as", "stands as", "functions as" = "is", "has", "does"

5. Check rhythm
   → All sentences same length? Vary them.

6. Check for rule of three
   → Lists forced into 3 items? Remove or expand.

7. Scan for filler phrases (tabel #22)
   → "In order to", "Due to the fact that" = simplify

8. Check for chatbot artifacts
   → "I hope this helps!", "Certainly!", "Let me know" = REMOVE

9. Add personality
   → Insert one opinion, one specific detail, one admission of uncertainty

10. Final read
    → Ask: "Would a real person write this?"
    → If no, rewrite that section.
```

## Platform-Specific Notes

### LinkedIn (Professional, tapi casual acceptable)
- Hook: Strong first line. No "In today's rapidly evolving..."
- Length: 1300 chars max per post
- Tone: Conversational professional. Opinionated.
- Avoid: Formal过度 — "I wish to inform you that..."

### Threads / Twitter (Casual, punchy)
- Hook: First 20 chars = make or break
- Length: 280 chars (Twitter), 500 chars (Threads)
- Tone: Authentic voice, not corporate
- Avoid: Thread-a-like language — each post must stand alone

### Blog / Long-form
- Opening: Hook or specific scene, not abstract intro
- Body: Varied rhythm, subheadings (Sentence case)
- Closing: Specific next step or question, not generic "hope this helps"

### Documentation / Technical
- Clarity over style
- Active voice
- Short sentences in sequence
- No promotional language
