## Content Strategy Notes

### What Works (from @gn.hermes analysis)
- ✅ Second person "lu/lo" — intimate, direct
- ✅ 2-5 sentences max — punchy
- ✅ Specific proof points (850+, 38→81, Rp 149rb)
- ✅ Natural CTA — "coba aja", "worth it"
- ✅ Honest/self-deprecating — "gw pernah", "sialan"
- ✅ Pain point first, solution second

- ❌ Too many emoji
